Become a Producer!

So you want share your own content in Nablem? Then you'll need to become an official Nablem Producer. Do not worry, the process is easy and straight-forward.

Benefits of becoming a Producer

    We'll spare your wallet. Becoming a Producer is toll free!
    You'll be able to upload your own content to our platform.
    People will be able to interact with your public content.
    You'll be one step closer to become a partner.
    You'll receive a special tag and badge in our platform.

Our Producers

Nablem is a home for creativity and passion to evolve, and all of our Producers share the same passion: produce content for people. We thank them all for such a hard and inspiring work. You can be part of the team as well!

Important Rules

All of this is AWESOME. But Nablem heavily worries about our community safety, so we would like to pass through some important rules that every Producer must follow in order to continue producing for our platform:

  1. Do NOT share content that violates our content policy.
  2. Do NOT attack, harm or discriminate your viewers based on their reviews and comments.
  3. ALWAYS accept constructive criticism, small reviews and even bad reviews. All of them will help you improve.
  4. Do NOT share content that isn't yours.
  5. Do NOT promote other websites or services without explicit permission. You can become a partner to do so.

Any rule breaking will result in warnings, account suspension or even permanent ban, based on your past records or the severity of the situation.
